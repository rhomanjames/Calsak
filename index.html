Lasers

Lasers have revolutionized plastics fabrication, providing precise cutting, engraving, and marking techniques. They eliminate waste, save time, and offer versatility for intricate designs. Using controlled heat, lasers ensure clean edges and enhance efficiency in manufacturing. Calsak plastics invests greatly in laser capabilities and expert machinists to provide our customers with the solutions that lasers make possible.

Die Cutters

Calsak Plastics utilizes the sharp blades and customizable dies that allow die cutters to create intricate and consistent shapes, making them essential tools for manufacturing processes and crafting projects.

Ovens/Forming Equipment

This form of fabrication refers to transforming a plastic sheet into a three-dimensional shape using heat, vacuum, and pressure. Calsak Plastics has identified many advantages to thermoforming and has invested in its capabilities. Heat Bending Tables

Heat bending is a popular process among Calsak Plastics customers due to its low tooling costs, quick set-up process, and consistency of results. Heat bending can achieve multiple angles and configurations based on customer requests. The heat-bending process allows thermoplastic sheets to be re-molded or formed. Acrylic, polycarbonate, PETG, ABS, and PVC are great material options for heat bending.

Gluing/Solvent Welding

Gluing, also known as solvent welding, is a common technique used in plastic fabrication and here at Calsak Plastics in Charlotte to join two or more plastic pieces together. By applying a solvent that dissolves the surface of the plastic, the molecules fuse and create a strong bond, resulting in a seamless and durable connection. Gluing or solvent welding offers a reliable and efficient method for assembling plastic components in various industries, from automotive and construction to electronics and packaging industries.
